Kale Banana Smoothie

Let's learn how to make this refreshing Kale Banana Smoothie!

Leafy greens are blended with almond milk, peanut butter, chia seeds, and a frozen banana in this vibrant kale smoothie.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 16 fluid ounces coconut water, chilled
  • 1 banana
  • ½ avocado, peeled and pitted
  • ½ cup packed kale
  • ⅛ lemon, juiced
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Step-By-Step Instructions

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect to do:

  1. Put coconut water, banana, avocado, kale, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper in blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
